Privacy Policy for Music Trends GH

Music Trends GH ("the App") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use the App.

Data Collection

The App collects the following information:

  • User authentication data (e.g., access tokens) for accessing TikTok data.
  • TikTok video data (e.g., hashtags, song titles, and metrics) for analytical purposes.

Data Use

The collected data is used solely for:

  • Analyzing TikTok video trends and song usage.
  • Providing users with insights based on TikTok data.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties, except as required by law or to comply with TikTok API policies.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
